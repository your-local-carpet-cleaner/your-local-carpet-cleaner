Get Your Full Bond Back

Carpet cleaning is one of the most common reasons Sydney tenants lose part of their bond. Most NSW leases require carpets to be professionally cleaned at the end of the tenancy — and real estate agents expect to see a dated, itemised receipt from a professional service, not a DIY attempt.

We have extensive experience with end-of-lease cleaning across Sydney, Sefton, Prestons, Revesby, and surrounding suburbs. We know exactly what property managers and real estate agents look for, and we clean to that standard every time.

Our bond-back guarantee means that if your agent flags the carpet cleaning specifically, we'll return within 72 hours and re-clean the identified areas at no extra charge. We want you to get your bond back — full stop.

Important

Under the NSW Residential Tenancies Act, landlords cannot require tenants to use a specific cleaning company. However, they can require professional cleaning (evidenced by a receipt) if the lease stipulates it. Our receipt meets all standard requirements.
