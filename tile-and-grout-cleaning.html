Tile & Grout Cleaning Sydney

Deep cleaning for tiled floors, kitchens and bathrooms across Sydney. We lift ground-in dirt and discolouration from grout lines that mopping alone can't shift.

Bring Tired Tile Floors Back To Life

Mopping cleans the surface of your tiles, but grout is porous — over time it absorbs dirt, grease and moisture, gradually turning from its original colour to a dull grey or brown that a household mop simply can't reach.

We use high-pressure hot water extraction to flush ground-in soil out of grout lines and lift grime from the surface of the tile itself, paired with pre-treatment on heavily soiled or stained areas. The result is a deep clean that restores the contrast between tile and grout — not just a surface wipe-over.

We work on kitchens, bathrooms, laundries and living areas across Sydney, Sefton, Prestons, Revesby and surrounding suburbs, on most common hard floor tile types.
