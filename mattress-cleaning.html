Your Mattress Needs Cleaning — Here's Why

You spend roughly a third of your life in bed. Over time, your mattress accumulates dead skin cells (which dust mites feed on), sweat and body oils, dust mites and their waste, pet dander if pets share the bed, bacteria, and potentially mould spores in humid Sydney conditions.

A mattress protector helps but doesn't address what's already embedded in the foam and fabric layers. Studies suggest a mattress can double in weight over 10 years due to accumulated organic matter. Professional cleaning removes this build-up and significantly reduces allergen load.

If you or a family member suffers from asthma, hay fever, or eczema, mattress cleaning can make a genuine difference to sleep quality and symptom management. We use a HEPA-filtered dry steam process that kills dust mites on contact without chemicals, followed by UV sanitisation for bacteria.
