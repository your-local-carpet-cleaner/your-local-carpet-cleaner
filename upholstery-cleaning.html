Upholstery Cleaning Sydney

Gentle yet highly effective upholstery cleaning for sofas, armchairs, dining chairs, ottomans, and more. Safe for all fabric types — including velvet, microfibre, and natural fibres.

Professional Upholstery Care in Sydney

Your sofa and armchairs absorb everything — sweat, skin cells, pet dander, food crumbs, dust mites, and airborne bacteria — every single day. Regular cushion-flipping only hides the problem. Professional upholstery cleaning removes it entirely.

We use a low-moisture, fabric-safe cleaning process that delivers a deep clean without saturating the padding or risking shrinkage. Every piece of furniture is assessed for fibre type and dye stability before we start, and we use the appropriate cleaning method accordingly.

Whether you've got a white linen sofa that's seen better days, a velvet chaise covered in pet hair, or a full dining set with years of built-up grime, we've seen it all — and restored it all.
