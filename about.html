Our Story

Family-owned and operated for over 20 years. Sydney's most trusted carpet cleaning specialists.

Family Owned & Operated

20 Years of Craftsmanship in Sydney

Your Local Carpet Cleaner was founded over two decades ago with a single van and an unwavering commitment to doing honest, quality work. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted household name across Sydney — but one thing has never changed: every job is done personally, with pride.

We are not a franchise. We don't send sub-contractors or cut corners on products. When you book with us, you get the same experienced hands that have been restoring Sydney carpets since 2004. That personal approach is why our customers keep coming back — and why 100% of our Facebook reviewers recommend us.

Our clients range from young families in Revesby and Sefton worried about pet stains, to landlords in Prestons needing a spotless end-of-lease clean, to offices across the CBD that want after-hours commercial maintenance. Whatever the job, we bring the same meticulous standards.
