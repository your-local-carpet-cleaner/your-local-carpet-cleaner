When Regular Cleaning Isn't Enough

Some stains and odours are resistant to conventional carpet cleaning. Pet urine, for example, soaks through carpet fibres into the backing and underlay, where bacteria break down the uric acid and produce the distinctive, persistent ammonia smell. Surface-only cleaning doesn't reach the source.

Our enzyme-based treatment targets these organic compounds directly — breaking them down at a molecular level rather than masking the smell with fragrance. The result is genuine odour elimination, not temporary suppression.

Similarly, red wine, coffee, blood, and grease stains that have been allowed to set — or that have been partially treated with DIY products — require professional-grade spotting agents and the right technique to remove without damaging fibres.

We offer emergency same-day service for fresh spills, where fast treatment dramatically increases the chance of full removal. Call us immediately if it just happened.

Pet urine soaks through carpet into the backing and underlay. If only the surface is cleaned, the uric acid crystals in the lower layers remain.
