Water Damage Restoration Sydney

Burst pipe, storm, or appliance overflow — when carpets and flooring get wet, every hour matters. We respond quickly with extraction and drying equipment to help limit the damage.

Fast Action Limits The Damage

Whether it's a burst pipe, an overflowing appliance, a leaking roof, or storm water finding its way inside, wet carpet and underlay don't just look bad — left untreated, they create the damp conditions that mould and odours need to take hold, often within 24–48 hours.

Our process focuses on getting moisture out fast: high-powered water extraction to remove standing water and saturation from carpet and underlay, followed by structural drying equipment to bring humidity down across the affected area. Where needed, we apply anti-microbial treatment to help guard against mould and bacterial growth while everything dries out.

We service homes and businesses across Sydney, Sefton, Prestons, Revesby and surrounding suburbs, and we'll always give you a clear, honest assessment of whether your carpet can be saved or needs replacing.
