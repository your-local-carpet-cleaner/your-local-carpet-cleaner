Carpet & Fabric Protection Sydney

A protective treatment applied after cleaning that helps your carpets, rugs and upholstery resist spills and stains for longer — and stay looking newer for it.

Buy Yourself More Time Before Stains Set In

Most fabric and carpet protectors used to wear off factory-applied finishes long before you'd expect — leaving fibres exposed to everyday spills, foot traffic and pet accidents that soak straight in and set fast.

After your clean, we apply a fabric-safe protective treatment that forms an invisible barrier across the fibres. Spills are far more likely to sit on the surface for longer — giving you the crucial extra minutes to blot them up before they soak in and become a permanent stain. It also helps slow down everyday soiling, so your carpet, rug or upholstery stays looking fresher between professional cleans.

It's a popular add-on for households across Sydney, Sefton, Prestons, Revesby and surrounding suburbs with young kids, pets, or simply high-traffic living areas.
